Building innovative minds for a sustainable digital future.

Who We Are

BIMNAR ICT is a growing technology education and innovation hub founded to empower students, teachers, and young minds through hands-on digital skills. Based in Kaduna, Nigeria, we specialize in practical training that prepares students for real-world problem-solving in the digital era.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to inspire innovation and creativity among students by equipping them with 21st-century digital skills. We envision a world where every student has access to quality tech education, can build impactful solutions, and contribute to sustainable development.

Our Focus on SDG 9

BIMNAR ICT aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure) by fostering a culture of innovation, promoting inclusive technology education, and supporting schools in integrating digital literacy into their curriculum.
