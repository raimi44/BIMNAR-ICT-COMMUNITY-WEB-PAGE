About BIMNAR ICT

BIMNAR ICT is a forward-thinking tech education initiative based in Kaduna state, Nigeria. Empowering businesses and individuals through innovative solutions, expert guidance, and creative collaboration, driving growth and success in a rapidly changing world and We are passionate about equipping students and Individuals with essential digital skills such as Web Development, Data Analysis, Python Programming, aligning with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure).

Introducing BIMNAR TechX 2025

BIMNAR TechX is a multi-school innovation challenge hosted by BIMNAR ICT CUMMUNITY. The program brings together students from schools to learn, create, and compete in technology-driven projects focused on web and game development.
